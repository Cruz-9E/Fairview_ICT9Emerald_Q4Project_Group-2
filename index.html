Who are we?

Pastee is a rising pastry business that originated in the Philippines,
coming from a group of aspiring young men who wanted to get rich ,
help others, and inspire more.


───────────────────



Our History


December 18, 2023 January 18, 2024 February 19, 2024 February 29, 2024
On December 18, a group of young fellows decided to share a dream—to own a business. After many debates, they settled on a food business named “Skibs.” They promised to support each other to reach their dreams. By January 18, the team finalized their brand identity and started testing product ideas. They gathered feedback and refined their recipes. On February 19, they launched their first batch of pastries to a small market. The response was positive, and they began expanding their reach. February 29 marked their first online sale. Pastee officially became a business with vision and momentum toward bigger goals.
